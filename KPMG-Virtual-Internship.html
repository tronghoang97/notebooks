By: Aaron Nhu & Hoang Vu

Introduction¶

This is the project hosted by KPMG Virtual Internship Program. Our tasks are to clean and explore one of KPMG's clients, Sprocket Central Pty Ltd, a medium size bikes & cycling accessories organisation. In this report, we will dive into the company's data and look for key intelligence to make suggestions that can help to improve the company's performance in the future.

In conclution, the company should:¶

  • Invest in advertisement in both gender
  • Try to push more sales in Touring and Standard bikes as they generate the highest profit margin
  • Focus on the brands generaing the highest profit margin: WeareA2B, Trek Bicycles, and Solex
  • Not focus solely on a certain range of age since the profit margin is the same across ages
